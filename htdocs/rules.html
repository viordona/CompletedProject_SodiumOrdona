Rules of the Game

Brain Rush is a quiz competition where you have to correctly answer a series of multiple-choice questions in order to advance to the next level/question. There are 15 questions in total and each question is worth a specific amount of money, and a 25 second limit decreasing by increments of 5 per level is placed for you to come up with an answer.

The questions are structured according to 3 different levels with each level increasing in difficulty. Each level contains 5 questions. Questions that are grouped into the same level will all be of similar difficulty. For a fair game, you are not allowed to switch tabs while playing. You will be given a warning if detected. Once you reach 5 warnings, you will receive a game over.

It is important to remember that the questions which make up each level will not necessarily relate to the same or even similar topics, but their overall level of difficulty will be the same. You will accumulate money with each correct answer, but if you answer incorrectly before reaching a safe haven, you will stand to lose a large amount of winnings. All scores and information will only be stored and displayed on the leaderboard page when the game is finished. Below is a summary of the question value structure:

Question Structure:

Level 1

  • Question 1: ₱ 100
  • Question 2: ₱ 200
  • Question 3: ₱ 300
  • Question 4: ₱ 500
  • Question 5: ₱ 1,000 (Safe Haven)

If you get question 5 wrong, you leave with nothing. If this question is answered correctly, you are guaranteed ₱ 1,000 even if you answer incorrectly before reaching the next safe haven at Question 10.


Level 2

  • Question 6: ₱ 2,000
  • Question 7: ₱ 4,000
  • Question 8: ₱ 8,000
  • Question 9: ₱ 16,000
  • Question 10: ₱ 32,000 (Safe Haven)

If you get question 10 wrong, you leave with ₱ 1,000. If this question is answered correctly, you are guaranteed ₱ 32,000 even if you answer incorrectly before reaching the next safe haven at Question 15.


Level 3

  • Question 11: ₱ 64,000
  • Question 12: ₱ 125,000
  • Question 13: ₱ 250,000
  • Question 14: ₱ 500,000
  • Question 15: ₱ 1,000,000 (Safe Haven)

If you get question 15 wrong, you leave with ₱ 32,000. If this question is answered correctly, then congrats! You have beaten the game and become a millionaire!


The game is aimed at knowledge enthusiasts who love an intellectual challenge. We invite you to play and earn your first million dollars with the power of your mind!

**Please confirm that you have read the rules. The game is made for Google Chrome. Any other browser may result in the loss of some features and elements moving out of place. You must confirm that you are using Google Chrome for the full experience.

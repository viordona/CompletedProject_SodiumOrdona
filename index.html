Welcome

Turn your knowledge into virtual wealth!

Brain Rush is a free online game that gives you the opportunity to make easy virtual money if you correctly answer some (not always easy) questions. It is a fantastic way to build your general knowledge and, if you are successful, make an easy ₱ 1,000,000 virtual pesos!

To succeed and reach the ultimate 15th question, you will need a clear understanding of how the game works. Please carefully read the rules of the game. Note that there are also consolation prizes, so even if you do not make it all the way to your first million, you will still have a lot of fun and be rewarded.

This game is free of charge, and best of all, you do not need to download it. You can play it online at any time. It is recommended to use Google Chrome.

Should you have any questions, comments, or suggestions related to this site, please feel free to contact us. We would enjoy hearing from you!

Quick Rundown:

The game consists of 15 questions. The level of difficulty increases with each question, and so does your potential wealth. For example, if you answer the first question correctly, your prize money goes from ₱ 100 to ₱ 1000. As you work your way through the questions by supplying the correct answers, your riches exponentially grow until you reach the final question - and if you answer it correctly, you will succeed in making a million dollars!

So, keep your wits about you, have fun, and get rich!

Good luck!
